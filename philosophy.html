The Philosophy of Decentralization

Basic Philosophy

Contemporary social media services operate on a highly centralized model called a 'silo'. These 'silos' are large data warehousing operations that gather all of the relationships, activity, and other content produced by the users of their systems into vast, nuanced collections of information about each user on the system, who they interact with, and upon what basis.

As you may recall from your End User Agreement (you did read that, right?) you have granted the operators of the service the practical and functional ownership of any content you produce or introduce to the system. In effect, YOU are THEIR content.

This is not an accident. Anything, no matter what, is valuable in sufficient quantities. And mountains of personal information about vast numbers of users of digital networks does not differ in this regard.

At KonnektFi, we think that the time has come for users not just to take their data back into their possession, but to continue that advance and accrue those benefits, financial and otherwise, back to themselves.
