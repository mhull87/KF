KNKT Token Overview

KNKT is an erc-20 token that will be bridged at a later time to achieve the greatest interoperability between the top networks. 50% of total supply will be released at launch. Every 3 months an amount voted on by the community will be released into circulation until all tokens are in circulation.

Tax Structure

Operations and community will benefit the most from the buy and sell taxes. Our tax structure is calibrated to support operational cost and successful longevity while still giving back to the community.

Liquidity

Liquidity is essential for any ecosystem to thrive. Without liquidity the ecosystem is unable to grow.

Buybacks

Buybacks will occur in intervals set by a community vote. Once these tokens have been paid for, they will then be burned to reflect that value back to the holders and support price stability. The buyback and burn function will serve to generate passive income for holders and stakers.

Staking

Staking is another way to generate even more passive income and will be paid in KNKT tokens. You may stake your tokens for 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, or 12 months. Each period will yield a different % of tokens for that period. You can unstake your tokens at any time but will forfeit the reward for doing so. You may make multiple, separate staking entries for different periods that will be independent of each other.

The dev wallets will be secured through a multi sig protocol. Gnosis Safe is one of the top providers for this feature. At least 4 out of 7 sig wallets must authorize any transactions that takes place from the development and operations, marketing, or treasury wallets.
