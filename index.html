Lite Paper

Superior Vision That Will Empower And Inspire!


Our decentralized ecosystem will redistribute revenue created by, or on, the platform through the KNKT token. KNKT is the main governance and utility token for the community, by the community. KNKT will have several functions. Users will be able to buy, sell, trade, or send KNKT to one another, as well as send them to any other wallet, platform, or network that supports the token.

  1. The purpose of a DAO is it is a community-driven and operated entity. The success, operation, and direction of the DAO is entirely dependent on the community. Holders of KNKT enjoy part ownership and governance of the KonnektFi ecosystem.
  2. KNKT is also utilized as a transactionary tool that holds monetary value. The community will be able to buy, sell, trade, or send the tokens on or off the platform as well as to each other. As with any currency, monetary value (price) will vary depending on several factors such as, supply and demand, circulating supply, and market cap, to name a few. Our tokenomics are set in such a way, that KNKT will have the characteristics of a deflationary token which helps stabilize the price or even increase the value of current tokens in circulation.
  3. KNKT is purchaseable by entities outside of the ecosystem in order to do business with or interact with our platform and community. Utilizing our token will allow for that revenue to be redistributed to the community, in effect creating a passive income for holders. As the community and holders increase, volume and market cap also increase. You could imagine that the KonnektFi app, with millions of users, could be just as valuable as any traditional platform but with one major difference! The holders of KNKT will benefit the most! By holding KNKT, transacting with one another, buying, selling, and trading on our platform, each user is contributing to the value and market cap of the whole ecosystem.

Development and Operations

This will be used for the continued development of our app as well as recurring operational costs.

Buybacks

Buybacks will occur in intervals set by a community vote. Once these tokens have been paid for, they will be burned to reflect that value to the holders and support price stability. The buyback and burn function will serve to generate passive income for holders and stakers.

Staking

Staking is another way to generate even more passive income and is paid in KNKT tokens. You may stake your tokens for one month, three months, six months, or 12 months. Each period will yield a different percentage of tokens for that period. You can unstake your tokens at any time but will forfeit the reward for doing so. You may make multiple, separate staking entries for different periods that will be independent of each other.
