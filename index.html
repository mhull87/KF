Tokenomics

The KNKT token is our main governance and utility token that will provide access to and governance for the ecosystem. Holders and stakers will possess voting rights. Our token allocation serves to protect community interest, investor security, and the ability to maintain a successful platform. We believe that community is the most important asset and our token allocation reflects our commitment to that belief. We also believe it is more important to build and maintain the best possible platform that will function properly and offer security which is why development and ops are our main priority. A good product will market itself. That is why our marketing allocation is lower than average. As with any project, it is important to acknowledge the founders that have made this project possible and are committed to the overall success of the whole community. We will conduct one presale to jumpstart momentum.

KNKT Token Overview

KNKT is an ERC-20 token that will be bridged at a later time to achieve the greatest interoperability between the top networks. 50% of the total supply will be released at launch. Every three months, an amount, voted on by the community, will be released into circulation until all tokens are in circulation.

Tax Structure

Operations and the community will benefit the most from the buy and sell taxes. Our tax structure is calibrated to support operational cost and successful longevity while still giving back to the community.

04% Development and Operations

Liquidity

Liquidity is essential for any ecosystem to thrive. Without liquidity, the ecosystem is unable to grow.

Buybacks

Buybacks will occur in intervals set by a community vote. Once these tokens have been paid for, they will be burned to reflect that value to the holders and support price stability. The buyback and burn function will serve to generate passive income for holders and stakers.

Staking

Staking is another way to generate even more passive income and is paid in KNKT tokens. You may stake your tokens for one month, three months, six months, or 12 months. Each period will yield a different percentage of tokens for that period. You can unstake your tokens at any time but will forfeit the reward for doing so. You may make multiple, separate staking entries for different periods that will be independent of each other.

Investor Protections

The dev wallets will be secured through a multi-sig protocol. Gnosis Safe is one of the top providers of this feature. At least 4 out of 7 sig wallets must authorize any transactions that take place from the development and operations, marketing, or treasury wallets.
