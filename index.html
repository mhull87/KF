Project Roadmap

KonnektFi will change the way we interact, by providing an ecosystem that will redistribute value and reward our daily interactions. This is the roadmap not just for the app, but for a social media revolution.

The Philosophy of Decentralization

Contemporary social media services gather all of the relationships, activity, and other content produced by the users of their systems into vast, nuanced collections of information about each user on the system, who they interact with, and upon what basis for their own fiduciary benefit.

At KonnektFi, we think that the time has come for us as users not just to take our data back into our possession, but to continue that advance and reserve those benefits, financial and otherwise, to our selves.

We, as service operators, intend to deliver a platform to make this possible.
