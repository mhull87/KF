KonnektFi Mission Statement

Mission:

At KonnektFi, we are building a platform that Konnekts our personal, social, and financial well being all together into one app. We are revolutionizing how we think about social media and the role it plays in our lives. KonnektFi will change how we interact by providing an ecosystem that will redistribute value and reward our daily interactions.

We are building social media 3.0

KonnektFi is a platform based on relationships, community, and purpose that will redistribute value back to the holders and users that create that value. Existing conglomerates horde most all of the wealth that these spaces create. Instead, our current everyday interactions could be earning a passive income for us, and KonnektFi is our gateway to unlock that potential.
